Biography

I am a PhD student at the Information Retrieval Lab (IRLab) at University of Amsterdam (UvA), supervised by Prof. dr. Maarten de Rijke , Dr. Herke van Hoof, and Dr. Harrie Oosterhuis. My research interests include reinforcement learning based recommender system, debiasing recommendations, and privacy preserving recommendations. Before joining UvA, I received my BE and ME degree in computer science from Renmin University of China under the supervision of Prof. dr. Xin Zhao and Prof. dr. Ji-Rong Wen in 2016 and 2019, respectively.
