Biography

I am a last-year Ph.D. candidate at the Information Retrieval Lab (IRLab) at University of Amsterdam (UvA), supervised by Prof. dr. Maarten de Rijke , Dr. Herke van Hoof, and Dr. Harrie Oosterhuis. Prior to that, I received my BE and ME degree in computer science from Renmin University of China under the supervision of Prof. dr. Xin Zhao and Prof. dr. Ji-Rong Wen in 2016 and 2019, respectively.

My research interest focuses on trustworthy intelligent information systems, with a focus on unbiasedness, fairness, robustness, and explainability.

I am about to finish my Ph.D. thesis titled Learning Recommender Systems from Biased User Interactions in Nov 2023, with the scheduled defense of my thesis on 7th Feb 2024. I am looking for positions to continue my research.
